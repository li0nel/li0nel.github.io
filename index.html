Hi! We are two passionate software engineers based in London. We help our clients designing their app or web-platform idea, defining the architecture and the technology stack, and then developing it or sometimes managing its outsourcing.

We appreciate that most entrepreneurs can hardly go and talk to freelance developers straight away, you really need someone who can write down a technical roadmap and make technical decisions for the MVP and each next release.
Unfortunately, if you go and talk to a freelance developer, say a WordPress developer, and ask him about which tech stack is the best for what you want to achieve... well.. it is a bit like asking a fishmonger if his fish is fresh!
We want to help your startup just like a CTO would do, picking up the right technology for your product and be there for you to support your project on the technological side.

We have 12+ cumulative years of full stack experience (front end, back end, database design, cloud or app design and deployment), all in startups, some as lead developer, co-founders and CTO, using all kind of technologies like Javascript, Go, C++, PHP, NodeJS, MongoDB, Python, Amazon Web Services etc
